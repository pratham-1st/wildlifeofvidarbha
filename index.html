Vidarbha

Vidarbha, in Maharashtra, is a hub of rich biodiversity, home to renowned reserves like Tadoba-Andhari, Pench, and Melghat. These forests support majestic Bengal tigers, leopards, sloth bears, gaur, and various deer species like chital and sambar.
